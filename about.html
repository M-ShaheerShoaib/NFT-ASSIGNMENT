Dreamers

Dreamers Is A Limited-Edition NFT Collection By Dubai-Based Digital Artist And Art Director Yugal Odhrani, Exploring The Intersection Of Fashion, Technology, And Dystopian Aesthetics. Launched In November 2021, The Collection Comprises 44 Meticulously Handcrafted 3D Portraits, Each Representing A Unique Avatar That Embodies Resilience And Imagination In A Speculative Future World.

Concept And Themes

At Its Core, Dreamers Reflects Odhrani's Fascination With Human Anatomy, Fashion, And The Emotional Depth Of Digital Characters. The Collection Envisions A World Where Individuals Don Minimalist Garments Designed To Address Complex Global Challenges, Symbolizing Survival And Adaptability In A Dystopian Environment. Each Avatar Serves As A Narrative Piece, Inviting Viewers To Explore New Ideas And Technologies Within A Reimagined World.

Artistic Style And Influences

Odhrani's Work Is Characterized By A Blend Of Surrealism And Realism, Drawing Inspiration From Classical Art And Contemporary Fashion. His Digital Portraits Convey A Range Of Human Emotions—Despair, Eccentricity, And Authenticity—Through Detailed Facial Expressions And Body Language. This Fusion Of Traditional Artistic Elements With Modern Digital Techniques Positions Odhrani As A Notable Figure In The NFT Art Space.

Availability And Collectibility

With Only 44 Pieces In Existence, Dreamers Is A Rare Collection That Has Garnered Attention For Its Artistic Value And Conceptual Depth. As Of April 2022, 32 Editions Have Been Released, With A Limited Number Available For Purchase On Platforms Like OpenSea And Nifty Gateway.
